Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Assembled peanut butter and jelly sandwich.

The original, the O.G., the one you brought for lunch in a brown bag. The three-ingredient go-to that never goes out of style. Behold: The classic PB & J.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (your choice)
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Place the two pieces of bread on the plate.
  2. Spread peanut butter on one piece, and jelly on the other.
  3. Place the two pieces of bread together into a sandwich shape.
